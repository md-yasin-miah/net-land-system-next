This document outlines the technical specifications for the R-Series Logic Controllers used in industrial automation environments. Please ensure all hardware revisions are checked before deployment.

1.0 Power Requirements

The R-Series operates on a standard 24V DC input. Variations in voltage exceeding +/- 5% may result in unexpected reboot cycles or communication timeouts on the fieldbus interface.
