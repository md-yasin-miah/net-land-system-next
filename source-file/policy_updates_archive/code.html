Policy Updates & Change Log

calendar_today Last Updated: Oct 24, 2023 person Revised by Legal Dept.

Net Land is committed to maintaining transparency with our partners and customers. This document outlines the recent modifications made to our global Warranty, Return, and Shipping terms. All changes listed below are effective as of November 1, 2023.

info

Summary of Key Changes

  • Extended standard warranty from 12 to 24 months for Premium Series.
  • Updated return shipping window to 30 days (previously 14).
  • Revised international customs declaration procedures for APAC region.
verified_user

1. Warranty Policy Updates

Section 1.4: Coverage Duration

"All hardware components are covered for a period of twelve (12) months from the date of original purchase under normal operating conditions."
add_circle "Net Land Premium Series hardware components are now covered for a period of twenty-four (24) months. Standard series remains at twelve (12) months."

Reason for change: Alignment with new industry standards and increased hardware reliability metrics.

Section 2.1: Exclusions

Addition to Exclusions List:

"Damage resulting from unauthorized third-party firmware modifications or 'rooting' of Net Land networking interfaces is strictly excluded from warranty coverage."
