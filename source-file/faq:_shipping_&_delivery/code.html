Shipping & Delivery

Find answers to common questions about our shipping processes, delivery timelines, and international logistics for enterprise gear.

Once your order has shipped, you will receive an email with your tracking number and a link to the carrier's website. You can also track your order directly from your Account Dashboard under 'Order History'. Simply click the 'Track Order' button next to your most recent purchase to see real-time updates.

Yes, we offer weekend delivery for most metropolitan areas. Standard Shipping typically delivers Monday through Friday, but our Expedited and Priority shipping methods include Saturday delivery at no extra cost in eligible ZIP codes. Sunday delivery is currently restricted to specific regions serviced by our local courier partners.

Shipping costs for enterprise-grade hardware are calculated based on weight, volume, and destination.

  • Domestic Freight: Starting at $149 per pallet.
  • International Logistics: Custom quotes provided at checkout.
  • White Glove Service: Includes inside delivery and debris removal for an additional $99 fee.
Orders over $10,000 qualify for complimentary standard freight shipping.

If your order has not yet been processed (usually within 2 hours of placement), you can update the address via the Self-Service Portal. If the order has already moved to 'Processing' or 'Shipped', please contact our 24/7 support team immediately. While we cannot guarantee changes once the item is with the carrier, we can often initiate a package redirect for a small carrier-imposed fee.
