Shipping & Delivery

How long does standard shipping take for hardware? expand_more
Most in-stock networking hardware is processed within 24 hours. Standard delivery typically takes 3-5 business days within the continental US. Express overnight options are available at checkout for urgent replacements.
Do you offer international shipping? expand_more
Yes, we ship to over 50 countries. Please note that customs duties, taxes, and import fees are the responsibility of the recipient. Shipping times vary significantly by region.

Product Compatibility

Are your SFP modules compatible with Cisco and Juniper? expand_more
Our transceivers are MSA compliant and available with specific vendor coding for Cisco, Juniper, Arista, and more. Use our Compatibility Finder tool or contact a technical specialist to ensure you receive the correctly coded module.
Will this rackmount kit fit a 19-inch standard rack? expand_more
Yes, all of our rack-mount accessories are built to EIA-310 standard specifications for 19-inch rack frames unless explicitly stated otherwise in the product description.

Returns & RMA

What is your policy for DOA equipment? expand_more
Dead on Arrival (DOA) equipment must be reported within 48 hours of delivery. We will issue an immediate Advance Replacement and provide a prepaid return label for the faulty unit.
